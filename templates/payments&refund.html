Payments & Refunds

We ensure secure transactions and hassle-free refunds. Here's everything you need to know.

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment options:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • Net Banking: Available for major banks
  • Wallets & UPI: Google Pay, Paytm, PhonePe
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for selected locations

Refund Policy

We offer refunds in the following cases:

  • Defective or Damaged Products: Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Incorrect Items: Return the item within 14 days
  • Order Cancellations: Full refund for cancellations made within 12 hours
  • Non-Refundable Items: Personalized or final sale products

Secure Transactions

Your payments are encrypted and processed securely through trusted payment gateways.
