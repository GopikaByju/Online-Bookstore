Shipping & Returns

We ensure smooth and hassle-free shipping. Here's what you need to know about our shipping and return policies.

Shipping Policy

We process and ship orders within 24-48 hours on business days.

  • Domestic Shipping: 3-7 business days.
  • International Shipping: Varies by destination.
  • Tracking Information: Sent via email after shipping.

Return & Exchange Policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

  • Refunds: Processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Exchanges: Defective or incorrect items will be replaced.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Personalized or final sale items.

Order Cancellations

Orders can be canceled within 12 hours of placing them. After that, cancellations are not possible.
